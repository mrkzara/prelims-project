Our Mission

A holistic approach to education aims to develop the total person, encompassing physical, mental, emotional, spiritual, and social dimensions. By nurturing each of these areas, individuals become fully prepared to meet the challenges of life and contribute to the attainment of national development goals. The integration of these five elements results in a well-rounded individual who is equipped with not only academic knowledge but also life skills, personal values, and social responsibility. This approach recognizes the importance of developing individuals who can positively impact society by being socially responsible, culturally sensitive, and morally upright. As such, a center for holistic education seeks to produce individuals who can lead successful and fulfilling lives while contributing to the betterment of society.

Our Vision

Batangas College of Arts & Sciences, Inc (BCAS) is dedicated to creating an environment that fosters learning and personal growth for its students. With a focus on helping students achieve their potential, BCAS offers a supportive and challenging academic experience to prepare them for successful careers as Chartered Accountants. Through its programs, BCAS provides students with a comprehensive education that includes not only technical knowledge but also the skills and mindset necessary for professional success. By creating an environment that fosters personal growth and encourages academic excellence, BCAS aims to empower students to become well-rounded, ethical, and competent professionals in the field of accounting.
